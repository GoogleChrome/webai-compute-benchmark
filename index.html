This benchmark contains several interesting workloads for running AI models and machine learning tasks on the client side. The frameworks and models contain assets of considerable size. Currently, this downloads more than 2 GB of data in the course of the benchmark. Do not runt his on a metered connection, prefer a stable broadband connection. Also, the first iteration of the benchmark may take several minutes to complete.
